ACC 646 FORENSIC ACCOUNTING AND VALUATION

This course focuses on financial statement fraud. Skills taught in this course include using financial statement analysis to detect fraud in financial statements, and valuation skills that can be used to value both public and private firms in cases of shareholder and partnership disputes, divorce cases etc. Finally, students are given a chance to use their skills to detect and investigate fraud within a company's financial statements.
